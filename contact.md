## Directions

### By Public Transportation

**From Hangzhou East Railway Station:**
- Take Metro Line 1 to Ding'an Road Station, then transfer to Metro Line 2
- Or take bus routes 31, 108, or K155

**From Hangzhou Xiaoshan International Airport:**
- Take Airport Bus Line 1 to Wulin Square, then transfer to local transportation
- Taxi journey approximately 40-50 minutes

### Nearby Landmarks

- West Lake (Xihu): ~3 km
- Zhejiang University Yuquan Campus: ~2 km
- Hangzhou City Center: ~1 km
